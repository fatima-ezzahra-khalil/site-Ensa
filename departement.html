In Owr School We Have 4 Departments

mathematique & informatique

The Mathematical Computing Department combines math and computer science to solve complex problems using algorithms.

cyber-security

The Cyber Security Department focuses on protecting systems and data from cyber threats, teaching skills in encryption, ethical hacking, and risk management.

Electrical

The Electrical Engineering Department focuses on electrical systems, circuits, and technologies, preparing students for careers in power, electronics, and automation

Process

The Process Engineering Department focuses on optimizing and managing industrial processes for efficiency and sustainability.
